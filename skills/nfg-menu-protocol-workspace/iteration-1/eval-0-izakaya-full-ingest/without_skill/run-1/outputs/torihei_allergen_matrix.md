## High-risk flags for the concierge
1. **本日の刺身盛り合わせ (item 5)** — contents change daily. The concierge must NOT state a fixed allergen list; it should say the platter is a chef's daily selection of raw fish/seafood and direct allergy-sensitive diners to ask staff for the day's exact contents. Highest allergen-uncertainty item.
2. **Dashi (items 1 & 3)** — bonito (katsuo) dashi introduces a **fish** allergen and breaks vegetarian suitability. Kombu dashi does not. Confirming the dashi base resolves both questions at once.
3. **Karaage coating (item 2)** — wheat flour vs. potato starch (katakuriko) is the difference between a wheat-containing and wheat-free dish. Critical for celiac/wheat-allergy diners.
4. **Cross-contact** — shared fryer (karaage) and shared knives/boards (sashimi) are not captured above; confirm with owner.
