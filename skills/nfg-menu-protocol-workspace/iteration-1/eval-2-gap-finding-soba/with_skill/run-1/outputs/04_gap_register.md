The single most important fact to establish for a soba shop: **the buckwheat/wheat
ratio of the noodles.** Buckwheat (そば) is a severe, JP-mandatory allergen, and
whether the noodles are wheat-free (十割) or wheat-containing (二八 etc.) cannot be
guessed.
