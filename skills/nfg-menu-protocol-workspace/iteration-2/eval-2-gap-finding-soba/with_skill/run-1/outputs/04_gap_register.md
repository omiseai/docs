## A. Safety-critical (allergen / restriction) — must close before the concierge answers allergy questions

1. **[OPEN]** **Cross-contamination (whole shop)** — This kitchen handles buckwheat (そば)
   flour, a severe allergen. For a guest with a buckwheat allergy, can ANY dish be made
   safe, or do you share boiling water / surfaces / utensils across all dishes?
   a) all dishes share buckwheat-contaminated equipment (assume not safe)
   b) curry udon is cooked completely separately
   c) other → ______
   *Determines whether the concierge may ever say a dish is buckwheat-safe.*

2. **[OPEN]** **Noodle composition — そば (Dishes 1, 2)**: Are your soba noodles
   a) 二八 (contains wheat つなぎ, ~20%), b) 十割 (100% buckwheat, no wheat), c) other?
   *Determines wheat allergen + gluten-free status for zaru soba and tempura soba.*

3. **[OPEN]** **Tempura item — 天ぷらそば (Dish 2)**: What is the tempura on top?
   a) 海老 shrimp (→ shrimp/crustacean allergen), b) vegetables only (kakiage/野菜),
   c) mixed, d) varies → ______
   *Shrimp is a top-tier allergen; this must be pinned per the standard tempura soba you serve.*

4. **[OPEN]** **Tempura batter egg — 天ぷらそば (Dish 2)**: Does your tempura batter contain egg?
   a) yes (egg in batter), b) no (flour + water only), c) unsure → ______
   *Determines egg allergen.*

5. **[OPEN]** **Frying oil — 天ぷらそば (Dish 2)**: The tempura is fried in:
   a) pure vegetable oil, b) a blend containing **sesame (ごま油)**, c) other → ______
   *Determines sesame allergen.*

6. **[OPEN]** **Curry meat — カレーうどん (Dish 3)**: What meat (if any) is in the curry?
   a) none / vegetable, b) chicken, c) pork, d) beef, e) other → ______
   *Determines pork/beef/chicken allergens AND no-pork / no-beef / halal restrictions.*

7. **[OPEN]** **Curry roux dairy — カレーうどん (Dish 3)**: Does your curry roux/sauce contain
   milk or dairy (e.g. milk powder, cream)? a) yes, b) no, c) unsure → ______
   *Determines milk allergen + dairy-free.*

8. **[OPEN]** **Tsuyu / broth base — Dishes 1, 2, 4 (and curry broth, Dish 3)**: Your
   soup/dipping sauce uses:
   - soy sauce (醤油)? a) yes (→ soy + usually wheat), b) no, c) tamari/wheat-free soy → ______
   - dashi? a) かつお (bonito → fish), b) 昆布 only (kombu, no fish), c) blend incl. 煮干し
     (niboshi → fish) → ______
   *Determines soy / wheat / fish allergens and vegetarian/vegan status across most dishes.*

9. **[OPEN]** **Sobagaki accompaniment — そばがき (Dish 4)**: How is it served?
   a) plain, b) with soy-sauce dip, c) with dashi tsuyu, d) with wasabi-soy → ______
   And: does the sobagaki itself contain any wheat? a) no (buckwheat + water only), b) yes.
   *Determines whether sobagaki can be the wheat-free / vegan-friendly recommendation.*
